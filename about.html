Education

Undergraduate

I completed my undergraduate degree at Saginaw Valley State University in Saginaw, Michigan. I graduated in 2016 and received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Pyschology, minoring in Sociology.

Graduate Degrees

My first graduate degree is from Michigan State University in East Lansing, Michigan. I graduated in 2018 with a Masters in Social Work.

Work Experience

Prior to my work in libraries, I worked in the social work field. I provided in-home and in-school substance use therapy to clients, and then worked in a children's advocacy center. When I chose to leave the social work field, I happened upon libraries by accident. I reached out to my local library, the Lake Odessa Community Library, to see if they were hiring, and started working there part-time as a Library Assistant.

While working at the Lake Odessa Community Library, I realized that I wanted to make a career out of library work. In 2023, I began working as the Circulation Supervisor at the Hastings Public Library. At the beginning of 2025, I was promoted to Assistant Director at the Hastings Public Library.
